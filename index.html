Gioi thieu

Pexis la gi?

Pexis la nen tang B2B duoc thiet ke rieng cho nganh in an, ket noi truc tiep giua nha cung cap (lab, xuong in) voi mang luoi dai ly (studio, tiem anh, cua hang).

Du lieu luu tru local

Toan bo du lieu duoc luu tru tren may tinh cua nha cung cap voi SQLite. Khong phu thuoc vao cloud, khong lo mat du lieu.

Ket noi HTTPS bao mat

Su dung Cloudflare Tunnel de tao ket noi HTTPS an toan giua nha cung cap va dai ly. Khong can IP tinh hay mo port.

Toc do & hieu suat

Ung dung desktop duoc toi uu hoa de chay nhanh, me muot tren ca Windows va macOS. Thao tac tuc thi, khong do tre.

Cach hoat dong

Bat dau chi trong 4 buoc

Thiet lap he thong nhanh chong — tu cai dat den ket noi voi dai ly chi mat vai phut.

1

Cai dat PexisCore

Cai dat PexisCore tren may tinh cua nha cung cap. Server se tu dong khoi dong va tao database local.

2

Dang ky tren PexisAdmin

Dang ky tai khoan nha cung cap tren PexisAdmin. He thong se tao tunnel HTTPS tu dong.

3

Chia se ma NCC

Nha cung cap nhan duoc ma rieng. Chia se ma nay cho dai ly de ho ket noi vao he thong.

4

Dai ly ket noi

Dai ly cai PexisConnect, nhap ma NCC va bat dau dat hang, chat, gui file ngay lap tuc.

Tinh nang noi bat

Moi thu ban can, trong mot nen tang

Pexis duoc xay dung voi day du tinh nang de van hanh lab in an hieu qua va ket noi mang luoi dai ly.

Chat real-time

Trao doi truc tiep giua nha cung cap va dai ly. Gui tin nhan, hinh anh, file — tuc thi va bao mat.

Quan ly don hang

Tao, tiep nhan, xu ly don hang tu dai ly. Theo doi trang thai tu luc dat den khi giao hang.

San xuat & theo doi

Phan cong san xuat theo cong doan, cap nhat tien do real-time. Biet chinh xac don hang dang o dau.

Nhan su & cham cong

Quan ly nhan vien, ca lam viec, cham cong. Tinh luong tu dong dua tren du lieu cham cong.

Thu chi & cong no

Ghi nhan moi giao dich thu chi, theo doi cong no tung dai ly. Bao cao tai chinh ro rang.

Phan quyen tai khoan

Tao nhieu tai khoan voi quyen han khac nhau. Moi nhan vien chi thay nhung gi can thiet.

Ket noi HTTPS bao mat

Cloudflare Tunnel tao ket noi ma hoa HTTPS tu dong. Khong can IP tinh, khong can mo port router.

Du lieu local

Database SQLite chay tren may nha cung cap. Du lieu cua ban thuoc ve ban — khong phu thuoc cloud.
